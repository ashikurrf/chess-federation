9th January 2021

MUJIB Centenary Fide Chess Compltetion

Mr. Md Shafiqul Islam, BPM (BAR), Commissioner, Dhaka Metropolitan Police, inaugurated of the Mujib Birth Centenary Invitational FIDE Rating Chess Tournament 2021, as chief guest. General Shahidullah Chowdhury, ndc (Retd.)

Executive Director, Abul Khair Group were present as a special guest. K M Shahidullah Vice President of Bangladesh Chess Federation and Chairman, Tournament Committee presided over the ceremony and Syed Shahab Uddin, General Secretary of Bangladesh Chess Federation and President, FIDE Zone-3.2 gave speech, four Executive Member of Bangladesh Chess Federation Moniruzzaman Palash, Jakir Ahmed, IO Mahmuda Hoque Chwodhury Moly and Sajol Mahmud were present in the ceremony
